Wait! Do you actually need a visa?

Most British citizens only need an eTA ($7 CAD). But if you hold an Indian, Nigerian, or other non-exempt passport, you need a visitor visa.

Exemption Alert: US Green Card Holders

If you hold a valid US Green Card, you do NOT need a visa! You just need an eTA, even if your passport is from India or Pakistan.

01. Canada Visitor Visa Requirements

Applying for a Canadian travel visa application requires specific documents. Missing one usually means a delay.

Valid Passport

Must be valid for 6+ months. You will need to submit your actual passport for the stamp.

Financials

3-6 months bank statements showing £4,000 - £6,000 consistent balance.

Ties to UK

Employment letter or university letter proving you will return to the UK.

Bringing Parents or Grandparents?

Don't just apply for a standard visitor visa! The Super Visa allows parents and grandparents to stay in Canada for up to 5 years per visit (unlike the standard 6 months). Ask us about this special option.

03. How to Apply (Online)

  1. 1

    Upload Documents

    Create a GCKey account on the Canada.ca portal. Upload your PDFs (keep them under 4MB!). Pay the $185 CAD fees.

  2. 2

    Give Biometrics

    Within 24 hours, you get a letter. Book an appointment at VFS Global in London to give fingerprints.

  3. 3

    Passport Submission

    Wait 2-4 weeks. Once you get the "Request Letter", send your passport to get the 10-year stamp!

Top 2 Mistakes People Make

1. The "Funds Dump" Mistake

Transferring £5,000 into your account 2 days before printing statements. IRCC rejects this immediately. Funds must be in your account for 3+ months or have a clear source.

2. Hiding Travel History

Thinking a visa rejection from 10 years ago doesn't matter. It does. Canada shares data with the USA, UK, and Australia. Always declare past refusals.
