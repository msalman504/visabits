Do I Need an ESTA or a Visa?

Most US visas for UK citizens start with determining eligibility.

ESTA (Visa Waiver)

For British Citizens staying under 90 days.

  • Valid for 2 years
  • Fast Online Approval

B1/B2 Visitor Visa

For UK Residents (Indian, Pakistani, etc.) or long stays.

  • Valid for 10 years
  • Interview Required

Warning: Who is NOT Eligible for ESTA?

Even if you are a British Citizen, you cannot apply for an esta tourist visa usa if:

  • You have traveled to Iran, Iraq, Libya, North Korea, Somalia, Sudan, Syra, or Yemen on or after March 1, 2011.
  • You have traveled to Cuba on or after January 12, 2021.
  • You have a criminal record (even if spent).

In these cases, you MUST apply for a full B1/B2 united states tourist visa. Applying for an ESTA will lead to a permanent rejection.

02. Step-by-Step: Applying for a US Visa from UK

01

Complete the DS-160 Form

The DS-160 is the foundation of your united states tourist visa application. Every uk tourist visa for usa applicant (including children) must have their own DS-160.

Critical Tip: Be consistent! If you say you are visiting for "2 weeks" here, do not say "2 months" in your interview. Inconsistencies are the #1 reason us visas for uk citizens are denied.

02

Pay the Fee & Book Interview

The fee for a us tourist visa from uk is currently $185 USD. Once paid, you can schedule your appointment at the US Embassy in London or the Consulate in Belfast.

Wait Times: Slots for a visit visa to usa from uk can be scarce. We recommend booking at least 3 months in advance of your us vacation visa dates.

03

The Embassy Interview

This is the most stressful part of getting a visa for us for uk citizens. You must prove you have "strong ties" to the UK and will return after your trip.

  • Be Honest: Never lie about past arrests or refusals.
  • Bring Evidence: Bank statements, employment letters, and property deeds proving why you must return to the UK after your america tourist visa trip.
  • Dress Provisionally: Treat it like a job interview.

03. Common Questions (FAQ)

How long does the US Visa take?

For an ESTA, it takes 72 hours. For a B1/B2 Visa, it depends on interview slots in London. After the interview, your passport is usually returned with the visa in 3-5 working days.

Can I work in the USA on a Visitor Visa?

No. You cannot do paid work. You can attend meetings or conferences, but you cannot be employed by a US company.

What if my visa is denied?

The $185 fee is non-refundable. This is why it's critical to ensure your DS-160 and "ties to UK" evidence are perfect before applying.
