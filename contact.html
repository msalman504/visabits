Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does the visa application process take?

Processing times vary by country and visa type. Generally, tourist visas take 1-3 weeks, business visas 2-4 weeks, and cruise visas 1-2 weeks. We'll provide specific timelines for your application.

What documents do I need to provide?

Required documents vary by visa type and destination. Common requirements include passport, photographs, application forms, financial documents, and travel itinerary. We'll provide a complete checklist for your specific application.

When do I need to pay for the service?

We only charge after we've verified all your documentation is complete and accurate. This ensures you're never charged for incomplete applications and gives you confidence in our service.

What if my visa application is rejected?

While we maintain a 97% success rate, if your application is rejected, we'll help you understand the reasons and guide you through a reapplication process. We don't charge additional fees for reapplication guidance.

Do you provide support after visa approval?

Yes, we provide ongoing support and can help with visa extensions, renewals, and other travel-related services. We're here to support your travel needs beyond the initial application.
