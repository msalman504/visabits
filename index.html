Frequently Asked Questions

Common queries about our visa facilitation services.

When do I pay the service fee?
We operate on a transparent model. You only pay our service fee after we have verified your documentation and confirmed your application is ready for submission. There are no hidden upfront costs.
How long does the process take?
Processing times vary by destination. For example, Australian eVisitor visas are often approved within 48 hours, while US B1/B2 visas may take several weeks depending on interview availability. We provide accurate timelines during your initial assessment.
Do you handle corporate or group applications?
Yes, we specialize in enterprise mobility. We offer dedicated account management for businesses, handling bulk applications for teams, crews, and delegations with streamlined compliance checks.
Is my personal data secure?
Absolutely. We utilize enterprise-grade encryption for all data transmission and storage. Your personal documents are handled strictly for visa processing purposes and in compliance with GDPR regulations.
