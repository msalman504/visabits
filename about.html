Our Journey & Mission

We started VisaBits with a simple goal: to remove the stress from global travel. Today, we are the trusted partner for thousands of UK travellers.

Who We Are

VisaBits isn't just a processing agency; we are a team of travel enthusiasts and immigration experts who understand that behind every visa application is a dream holiday, a crucial business deal, or a long-awaited family reunion.

Based in London, we recognized a gap in the market for a service that combines **enterprise-grade security** with a **personal touch**. We don't just push paper; we guide you through the complexities of international immigration law, ensuring your journey starts before you even board the plane.

Our Promise

We operate on a strict "No Visa, No Fee" policy for most categories. You only pay our service fee once we've verified your documentation is flawless.

Our Impact

With over 1,500 successful applications and a 97% approval rate, our track record is our proudest asset.
