Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. We are committed to protecting your personal data.

Last Updated: October 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you fill out forms, request consulations, or communicate with us. This includes:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Nationality and visa preferences
  • Any other information you choose to provide

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process your visa applications and consultations
  • Send you technical notices and support messages
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Communicate with you about new services and offers

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as described in this policy or with your consent, such as with government visa authorities when processing your application.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience on our website. You can set your browser to refuse all or some browser cookies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Please contact us if you wish to exercise these rights.
