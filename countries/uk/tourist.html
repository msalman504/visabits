Common Questions

Expert answers to your UK visa queries

How long does it take?

Standard processing is currently 3-6 weeks from your biometric appointment. Priority services (5 days) and Super Priority (24 hours) are available for an additional fee.

Can I work in the UK?

No, the Standard Visitor Visa strictly prohibits paid or unpaid work. You can attend meetings and conferences, but you cannot be employed.

What if I have been refused before?

We specialize in complex cases. If you have a previous refusal, we will analyze the reasons and help you prepare a stronger application that addresses the concerns.
