About UK Tourist Visa

The UK Standard Visitor visa allows you to visit the United Kingdom for tourism, visiting family and friends, or short business trips for up to 6 months.

Tourism & Leisure

Visit famous landmarks, museums, and explore the rich history and culture of England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland.

Family Visits

Reunite with family and friends living in the UK. Perfect for special occasions, holidays, and quality time together.

Business Activities

Attend meetings, conferences, or conduct business activities. Note: You cannot work or receive payment from UK sources.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about UK tourist visa applications

How long can I stay in the UK on a tourist visa?

The Standard Visitor visa allows you to stay in the UK for up to 6 months. You can apply for longer stays in certain circumstances, but this requires additional documentation.

Can I work on a UK tourist visa?

No, the Standard Visitor visa does not allow you to work, study, or receive payment from UK sources. You can only engage in business activities like attending meetings or conferences.

Do I need travel insurance for the UK?

While not mandatory, comprehensive travel insurance is highly recommended. The UK has excellent healthcare, but medical costs can be expensive for visitors without insurance.

Can I extend my UK tourist visa?

Extensions are possible in exceptional circumstances, but you must apply before your current visa expires. You'll need to provide strong reasons and additional documentation.

What if my visa application is refused?

If your application is refused, you'll receive a detailed explanation. You can reapply addressing the concerns raised, or appeal the decision if you believe it was made in error.
