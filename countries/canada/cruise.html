Canada Cruise Entry Requirements

Most cruise passengers visiting Canadian ports require either an eTA (Electronic Travel Authorization) or a visitor visa, depending on nationality and cruise itinerary.

eTA for Cruise Passengers

  • Required for visa-exempt nationals
  • Valid for 5 years
  • Processing: minutes to 72 hours

Visitor Visa Required

  • For non-exempt nationals
  • Biometrics may be required
  • Processing: 2-6 weeks
