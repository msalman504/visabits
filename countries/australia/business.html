About Australia Business Visa

The Business Visitor Stream allows you to conduct business activities in Australia for up to 12 months. Perfect for meetings, conferences, negotiations, and business-related activities.

Business Meetings

Attend meetings, conferences, seminars, and business negotiations with Australian companies and organizations.

Market Research

Conduct market research, explore business opportunities, and establish business relationships in Australia.

After-Sales Support

Provide after-sales service, training, and technical support for equipment or services sold to Australian clients.

Eligibility Requirements

Ensure you meet all requirements before applying for your Australia business visa

Valid Passport

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Australia business visa applications

Can I work on an Australia business visa?

No, the Business Visitor Stream does not allow you to work or receive payment from Australian sources. You can only conduct business activities like meetings, negotiations, and after-sales support.

How long can I stay in Australia on a business visa?

The Business Visitor Stream typically allows stays of up to 12 months, but the exact duration depends on your specific business activities and the visa officer's decision.

Do I need a business invitation letter?

Yes, a business invitation letter from your Australian business partners or conference organizers is highly recommended and often required for business visa applications.

Can I attend conferences on a business visa?

Yes, attending conferences, seminars, and business meetings is one of the main purposes of the Business Visitor Stream visa.

What if my business visa is refused?

If your application is refused, you'll receive detailed reasons. You can reapply addressing the concerns raised, or seek professional advice to strengthen your application.
