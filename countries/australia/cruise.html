Australia Cruise Visa

Smooth entry for shore excursions and multiple Australian ports

Do I need a visa for an Australia cruise?

Most UK cruise passengers visiting Australian ports require an electronic visa (eVisitor) even if you do not disembark for long. We ensure your visa covers all scheduled ports and dates.

  • Multiple entries permitted for the duration of your cruise
  • Stay up to 3 months per entry
  • Processing typically 1–3 business days

What we'll need

  • Cruise itinerary (ports and dates)
  • Passport copy and photo
  • Basic health and character declarations
