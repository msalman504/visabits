Australia eVisitor Visa

The Australia eVisitor visa (subclass 651) is perfect for UK residents planning to visit Australia for tourism or business purposes. This electronic visa allows you to stay in Australia for up to 3 months per visit within a 12-month period.

Valid for up to 12 months
Stay up to 3 months per visit
Multiple entries allowed
Processing time: 1-3 business days

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Australia tourist visas

How long can I stay in Australia with an eVisitor visa?

The eVisitor visa allows you to stay in Australia for up to 3 months per visit within a 12-month period from the date of grant. You can make multiple visits during this time.

Can I work in Australia with a tourist visa?

No, the eVisitor visa does not allow you to work in Australia. It's strictly for tourism and short-term business activities. If you need to work, you'll require a different type of visa.

Do I need to visit an embassy or consulate?

No, the eVisitor visa is entirely electronic. You apply online and receive your visa grant notice via email. There's no need to visit an embassy or consulate.

What if my visa application is rejected?

While our success rate is 97%, if your application is rejected, we'll help you understand the reasons and guide you through a reapplication process at no additional cost.

Can I extend my stay in Australia?

The eVisitor visa cannot be extended. If you need to stay longer than 3 months, you'll need to apply for a different type of visa before your current visa expires.
