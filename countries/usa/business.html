USA Business Visa (B‑1)

Meetings, conferences, negotiations and after‑sales support

Overview

The B‑1 visa is ideal for short business trips to the United States. Validity can be up to 10 years with multiple entries; stays up to 6 months per visit.

  • Interview at the US Embassy required
  • Strong evidence of ties to the UK recommended
  • Invite letter/agenda helpful
