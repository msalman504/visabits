Do I need ESTA or a visa?

ESTA (Most common)

  • Up to 90 days stay per entry
  • Valid 2 years, multiple entries
  • Applies to most cruise passengers

B‑1/B‑2 Visa

  • For those not eligible for ESTA
  • Embassy interview required
  • Longer stays and flexibility
